Comprehensive Sports Rehab

Why Choose ARES for Sports Rehab?

Our sports physical therapists are athletes themselves who understand the demands of competitive sports. We combine evidence-based techniques with sport-specific training to ensure you return to play stronger than before.

Personalized Treatment Plans

Every athlete is different. We create customized rehab programs based on your sport, position, injury, and goals. Whether you're a weekend warrior or professional athlete, we'll get you back in the game.

Benefits of Sports Rehab

Faster Recovery

Our targeted therapies accelerate healing while ensuring proper tissue repair to prevent re-injury.

Pain Reduction

Advanced techniques to manage pain and inflammation without over-reliance on medications.

Improved Performance

Not just recovery - we identify and correct biomechanical issues to enhance your athletic performance.

Injury Prevention

Learn techniques and exercises to reduce your risk of future injuries specific to your sport.

What to Expect

Comprehensive Evaluation

We start with a thorough assessment of your injury, movement patterns, and sport-specific demands to create a precise diagnosis and treatment plan.

Personalized Treatment Plan

Your therapist will develop a customized program that may include manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, modalities, and sport-specific drills.

Progressive Rehabilitation

As you improve, we'll advance your program through phases from pain relief to strength training to sport-specific functional exercises.

Return to Sport Protocol

We'll guide you through a gradual return to play with criteria-based progression to ensure you're competition-ready.

Who Needs Sports Rehab?

Competitive Athletes

From high school to professional levels, we help athletes recover from injuries and return to their sport safely and effectively.

Weekend Warriors

Recreational athletes deserve expert care too. We'll help you get back to the activities you love without pain.

Youth Athletes

Specialized care for growing athletes to address injuries while considering growth plates and development.

Chronic Injuries

For nagging injuries that won't go away, we identify the root cause and create lasting solutions.

Common Conditions We Treat

Sprains & Strains

Ankle sprains, muscle strains, ligament injuries

Tendon Injuries

Tendonitis, tendinosis, Achilles issues

Post-Surgical Rehab

ACL reconstruction, rotator cuff repair, meniscus surgery

Overuse Injuries

Shin splints, stress fractures, tennis elbow

Joint Pain

Shoulder impingement, hip labral tears, patellofemoral pain

Concussion Management

Gradual return to sport after head injury
